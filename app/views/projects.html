BATTLESHIP
Android game application developed for my senior design class that is based on the popular guessing game Battleship. The objective of for this assignment was to create a mobile game that is easy to play for any patient with cerebral palsy. Integrating the ASK Scanning Library, my team and I created an experience that was simple to play for cerebal palsy patients with limited motor ability.
